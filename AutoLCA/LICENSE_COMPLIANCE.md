# Data License & Compliance

Triya.io is designed as a **data-agnostic functional modeling platform**. It does not include any third-party proprietary Life Cycle Inventory (LCI) databases in its source code or distribution.

## Bring Your Own Database (BYOD) Model
To ensure legal compliance and respect intellectual property rights, users must provide their own licensed datasets.

### Supported Professional Formats
The platform's ingestion engine is compatible with industry-standard formats, including:
- **OpenLCA (.zolca)**: Fully supported for hierarchical process mapping.
- **ISO/TS 14048 (JSON)**: Supported for granular flow characterization.
- **Zipped ILCD**: Supported for large-scale inventory synchronization.

### Licensing Responsibility
- **Proprietary Datasets**: If you use databases such as **ecoinvent**, **GaBi**, or **CarbonMind**, you are responsible for ensuring you have a valid commercial license for the specific version and usage (e.g., individual, educational, or corporate).
- **Public Datasets**: Users are encouraged to utilize high-quality public repositories such as the **USDA LCA Commons**, **GLAD (Global LCA Data Access)**, or **ELCD (European Platform on LCA)**.

### Strategic Intelligence
The "AI Scan & Conclude" feature is a **functional metadata analyzer**. It identifies technical patterns (e.g., "Steel Manufacturing Archetype") based on the structure of your uploaded datasets. It does not contain pre-stored proprietary data.
